Essential message

Essential message

There is but one choice available to me, and that is how I spend my time. Every moment in making this choice I have the ability to determine the quality of my life and the impact I can have.

I vow to treat all beings with kindness and compassion, to cultivate goodness, to give freely, to live presently, to speak truthfully and to live the best life possible.

During the day, remember to ask…

During the day, remember to ask…

How does this thing that I am doing with my time contribute to treating all beings with kindness and compassion, cultivating goodness, giving freely, living presently, speaking truthfully and living the best life possible?

  • My touchstone values

    My touchstone values

    Connection:
    Connection with the self, with others, with nature and with all living beings.

    Compassion:
    Compassion towards self, others, the earth and all living beings.

    Kindness:
    Treating all living beings with kindness, dignity, patience, generosity, love and respect.

    Openness:
    Constant open-mindedness.

    Intentionality:
    Being present, grateful and happy.

    Authenticity:
    Acting with integrity, honesty, humility and authenticity.

    Positivity:
    Living in joy, happiness and contentment. Cultivating that which is wholesome, and letting go of that which is unwholesome.

    Peace:
    Acting in peace, finding contentment with what is and living in inner harmony.

    Purpose:
    Making a positive contribution in meaningful work.

    Growth:
    All the time slightly better. Learning from my experiences, and continuing to learn.
